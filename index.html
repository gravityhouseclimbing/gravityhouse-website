Our Story

Born in the Valley

Gravity House was created to offer a year round place to move, challenge yourself, and connect with others. Whether you already enjoy training or want a new way to stay active, climbing builds strength, confidence, and new skills while bringing people together.

The idea started with a simple moment. I took my kids to a bouldering gym for the first time and the joy on their faces was unforgettable. Climbing comes naturally to children. It sparks courage, laughter, and teamwork. That day I realised we needed a place like this in the valley.

Together with my co-founder Cyril, an experienced wall builder and passionate climber, we are building a climbing and sports hub for the Bega Valley. A place for climbers, families, young people, locals, and visitors to move, learn, and connect.
