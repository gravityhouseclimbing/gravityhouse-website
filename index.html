What We Offer

The Gym

Whether you're a first-time or seasoned climber, Gravity House has something for you. 300m² of best-in-class bouldering walls with different routes for all levels.

Bouldering

No ropes, no gear, no fuss. Colour-graded problems from absolute beginner to expert. The heart of Gravity House.

All levels welcome

Training Area

A functional fitness gym designed for young climbers. Hangboards, campus rungs, yoga and stretching. Everything you need to send that project at Gravity House.

Strength & Conditioning

Kids Zone

A colourful, dedicated area for young climbers aged 4–12. Shorter walls, gentle angles and fun routes. Youth matters here.

Family Friendly

Café & Drinks

Come and hang for a different vibe from your local community. Good coffee, cold drinks, good vibes.

Our Story

Born in the Valley

If the red hills of Wallaga Lake don't fire you up for the free-form journey, take a walk up to the bouldering gym and watch these light up on a Friday night as the community comes together.

Gravity House is Bega Valley's first indoor climbing gym — born out of community passion and a belief that everyone deserves access to climbing, wellness, and social connection, no matter their age or ability.
