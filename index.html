Our Story

Born in the Valley

It started simply: a Wallagoot dad took his two young kids to a bouldering gym and watched them light up. He looked around the Bega Valley and saw nothing like it — so he decided to build it.

Marc and co-founder Cyril set out to fill a real gap. Not just a gym, but a place where the whole community could come together — families, athletes, beginners, and kids — and find something that feels like surfing or mountain biking: exciting, accessible, and deeply social.
